Work autonomously without asking for confirmation. Only pause and ask Yegor when there is a genuine fork — a decision with significant consequences where different choices lead to meaningfully different outcomes.

**Why:** Yegor prefers an autonomous agent that moves forward on its own, not one that constantly checks in for trivial progress updates.

**How to apply:** Complete tasks end-to-end. Report results proactively when done. Ask only when: (1) two+ valid approaches exist with non-trivial trade-offs, (2) an irreversible action could have large consequences, (3) requirements are genuinely ambiguous and guessing wrong would waste significant effort.
